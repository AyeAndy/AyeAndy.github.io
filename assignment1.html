Assignment 1: Press Fit Kit

For this assignment, I wanted to play around with a press-kit fit of some traingular shapes. I took a break from work a little bit to drive to Home Depot and bought myself some calipers so that I can measure some leftover Home Depot boxes that I had from my last move.

After the measurements, I created a rectangular subtractions to my traingular shapes that would measure out to the depth of cardboard boxes.

Here I am making sure that the corners are rounded, so that the press-fit parts would fit better.

Keeping in mind of the cardboard box dimension I have, I decided to divvy up my prints into 2 sessions, as shown in my illustrator file below.

With the parts laser cut, I constructed some random structures - trying to stack them as high as possible.
